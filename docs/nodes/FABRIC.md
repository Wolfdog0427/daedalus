# Nodes & Fabric — Daedalus Body

Describes how nodes join, live, and express themselves.

---

## Lifecycle

1. Discovery  
2. Self‑announcement  
3. Auto‑join request  
4. Pending  
5. Operator approval  
6. Trusted  

---

## Presence surfaces

Glow, heartbeat, health, continuity, capabilities, ribbons.

---

## Sovereignty

Nodes may request to join.  
Nodes may never join automatically.

---

## Failure

Degraded nodes surface visibly.  
No silent failures.

---

## Invariants

- No auto‑trust  
- No bypass of ConnectivityGate  
- Node presence always visible
