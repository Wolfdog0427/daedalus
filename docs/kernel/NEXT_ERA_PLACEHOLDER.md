This placeholder defines the requirements for such an era.

A new governance era requires:

- A new Kernel  
- A new Shell

- A new Halo, Crown, and Throne  
- A new Rite

A new era must:

- Define its own constitution  
- Define its own expressive grammar  
- Define its own epistemic stance  
- Define its own continuity semantics  
- Define its own node trust model  
- Define its own operator relationship  

A new era is not an extension.  
It is a successor.

---

## 3. Relationship to the sealed era

The sealed Kernel era remains:

- Intact  
- Immutable  
- Historical  
- Authoritative  

A new era does not replace it.  
It builds beside it.

---

## 4. When to create a new era

A new governance era is only justified when:

- The operator's needs fundamentally change  
- The expressive grammar must evolve  
- The epistemic stance must shift  
- The sovereignty model must be redefined  
- The continuity semantics must be reimagined  

These are constitutional changes, not incremental ones.
